01 About

Good design is
as little design
as possible.

I'm a multidisciplinary designer with a decade of experience building products and identities that endure. I believe in systems over decoration, function as the premise of form, and the quiet authority of well-considered typography.

My work spans digital product design, brand identity, editorial, and creative direction — often at the intersection of all four. Based in Sheffield, working globally.
