01 Introduction

I am a Senior Service Designer at GDS. I enjoy untangling complex problems in order to deliver tangible value. Prior to this, I was a multi-award-winning Product Manager within the private sector, specialising in Film & TV Localisation (Disney, Netflix, Sony). A sporadic if somewhat lapsed sideline in freelance Illustration completes my high-level back story.
